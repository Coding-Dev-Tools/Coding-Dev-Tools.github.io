Predict SaaS churn from Stripe data

Every churned customer was a signal you didn't catch in time. Payment failures, usage dropping, support tickets spiking — they were all in Stripe. We just weren't looking at them together.

SaaS Churn Predictor connects to Stripe and scores every customer 0–100 nightly. It separates voluntary churn — cancellations, downgrades — from involuntary churn — failed payments, expired cards. Your team acts on the right root cause instead of guessing.

Install

git clone https://github.com/Coding-Dev-Tools/SaaS-Churn-Predictor.git
cd SaaS-Churn-Predictor
npm install

What it does

  • Stripe-native sync: customers, subscriptions, invoices, and payments sync automatically. No CSV uploads, no manual mapping.
  • Nightly 0–100 risk scores with plain-English explanations of why each customer is at risk.
  • Voluntary vs involuntary churn classification with separate intervention paths.
  • Intervention pipeline: Recommended → Contacted → Responded → Saved/Lost with save-rate analytics.
  • Real-time dashboard: MRR at risk, tier distribution, funnel, save-rate trending.
  • Alerts via Resend email and Slack webhook integration.
  • CSV import for non-Stripe usage and support data.
  • MRR-weighted risk calculations so a $2,000/mo account gets priority over a $29/mo account.
